How to Use

Get started in just a few steps. Select your platform below.

1

Install & Launch

Run the installer. If you select "Launch immediately", the app will start and appear in your system tray (bottom-right corner, near the clock).

2

System Tray Icon

TurboDictate runs in the background. Click the tray icon to show the widget, or use hotkeys directly. The app pops up automatically when you start recording.

3

Use Hotkeys Anywhere

Press Alt+R for English or Alt+A for Arabic to start recording from any application.

4

Speak & Stop

Speak clearly, then press Tab or click Stop. Your text is automatically copied to clipboard and pasted where your cursor is!

1

Enable Keyboard

After installing, open the app and tap "Enable Keyboard". Toggle ON "TurboDictate Voice" in settings.

2

Allow Permission

Android shows a warning for all keyboards - this is normal. Tap "OK" to continue. We don't collect any data.

3

Switch to TurboDictate

In any app, long-press the mic/globe icon on your keyboard, select "Input method", then choose "TurboDictate Voice".

4

Start Dictating!

Tap the mic button to record, tap again to stop. Your speech is transcribed and pasted instantly!
