Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

At TurboDictate, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information. We believe in transparency and keeping things simple.

📊

1. Information We Collect

TurboDictate collects minimal data necessary to provide voice-to-text transcription:

  • Audio Recordings: Temporary voice recordings captured when you use the dictation feature
  • Transcription Text: The converted text from your voice recordings
  • Language Preference: Your selected transcription language (English or Arabic)
⚙

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Audio recordings are sent to our transcription service to convert speech to text
  • Transcription history is stored locally on your device for your convenience
  • Language preferences are used to improve transcription accuracy

✅ Audio Deletion Guarantee

Your audio recordings are immediately deleted after transcription is complete. We do not store, retain, or archive any audio files on any server.

📡

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: All transcription history is stored exclusively on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not upload or sync your transcriptions to any cloud server
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses secure HTTPS encryption
  • Your Control: You can delete your transcription history at any time within the app
🌐

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service to provide transcription functionality:

🤖

Groq API

Speech-to-text transcription powered by Whisper

View Groq's Privacy Policy ↗

⚠️ Important Note

When you record audio, it is transmitted to Groq's servers for processing. Please review Groq's privacy policy to understand how they handle data.

🔒

5. App Permissions

TurboDictate requires the following permissions:

  • Microphone: Required to record your voice for transcription
  • Internet: Required to send audio to the transcription service
  • Storage: Required to temporarily save audio files before processing
👥

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your transcriptions in the History tab
  • Delete: Remove individual transcriptions or clear all history
  • Portability: Copy any transcription to use elsewhere
  • Opt-out: Simply uninstall the app to remove all local data
👶

7. Children's Privacy

TurboDictate is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

📝

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
